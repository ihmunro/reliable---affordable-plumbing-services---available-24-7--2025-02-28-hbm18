About PureFlow

Founded in 2010, PureFlow began with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable plumbing services to our community. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the region's most trusted plumbing companies, serving thousands of satisfied customers. Our journey has been built on the foundation of technical excellence and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Today, we remain dedicated to our core values of integrity, professionalism, and innovation. Our team of licensed plumbers undergoes continuous training to stay ahead of industry advances, while maintaining our commitment to transparent pricing and exceptional service. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers by delivering quality workmanship and reliable solutions for all their plumbing needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

We provide comprehensive plumbing services throughout the greater metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs, covering a 50-mile radius from the city center. All service areas receive the same rapid response times and quality service guarantees.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

Our emergency response team is available 24/7 with an average arrival time of 45 minutes. We maintain fully-stocked service vehicles throughout the service area to ensure rapid response to urgent plumbing issues.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and checks. For larger projects, we offer flexible financing options with approved credit. All payment terms are discussed upfront before any work begins.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, all our repairs and installations come with a comprehensive warranty. We offer a lifetime warranty on workmanship and honor all manufacturer warranties on parts and equipment we install.

Are your plumbers licensed and insured?

All our plumbers are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. They undergo regular training and certification updates to stay current with the latest plumbing technologies and safety standards.
